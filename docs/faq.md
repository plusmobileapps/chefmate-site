# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

### What is Chef Mate?
Chef Mate is a cooking companion app that helps you discover recipes, plan meals, and generate grocery lists.

### Is Chef Mate free to use?
Chef Mate is free to download with optional premium features available through a subscription.

### What platforms is Chef Mate available on?
Chef Mate is available on iOS and Android.

## Account

### How do I create an account?
Download Chef Mate from the App Store or Google Play and follow the on-screen sign-up instructions.

### How do I delete my account?
You can delete your account from any of the desktop, Android, or iOS apps:

1. Go to **Settings**
2. Select **Manage Profile**
3. Click **Delete Profile**
4. Confirm the deletion when prompted

Once confirmed, your account and all associated data will be permanently removed.

## Features

### Can I save my favorite recipes?
Yes — tap the heart icon on any recipe to save it to your favorites.

### Can I share recipes with friends?
Yes — use the share button on any recipe to send it via text, email, or social media.

## Support

### How do I report a bug?
Please contact us at **support@chefmateapp.com** with a description of the issue and your device information.
